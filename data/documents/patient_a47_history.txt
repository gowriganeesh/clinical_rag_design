There is no history of diabetic retinopathy or nephropathy; the annual eye exam and urine microalbumin were within normal limits. The patient reports occasional tingling in the feet, and a monofilament test suggested early peripheral neuropathy. Foot care advice was given along with reinforcement of regular glucose monitoring.
